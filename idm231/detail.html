Kim Petras

Virgo - Aug 22 - Sep 21

August 27, 1992

Kim Petras is a German singer, model and songwriter, currently based in Los Angeles. Since 2016, Petras has been releasing music as an independent artist under her own imprint, BunHead Records. Petras began recording music as a teenager, releasing her debut extended play One Piece of Tape in 2011
